Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services, fees, programs, and facilities. If you still have questions, don't hesitate to contact our team!

How much does tutoring cost?

Tutoring fees start from Rp 300,000/month for the regular package, with a 50% discount for this month's registrants. We also offer intensive packages (Rp 450,000/month) and premium packages (Rp 600,000/month) according to your needs.

Is there a results guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you are not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days. In addition, 95% of our students see improved grades within the first 3 months.

How does the payment system work?

Payments are made monthly with various methods: bank transfer, e-wallet (OVO, GoPay, Dana), or cash at our office. 0% installment plans are available for packages of 6 months or more.

How long is the program duration?

Program duration is flexible according to your needs. A minimum of 3 months is recommended to see significant improvement. Intensive exam preparation programs usually last 6-12 months, while regular programs can be ongoing as desired.

Can the class schedule be changed?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling with at least 24 hours' notice. For premium packages, schedules can be adjusted at any time to suit your family's needs.

Are online and offline classes available?

Yes, we provide both online and offline (in-person) classes. You can choose the learning method that best suits your child's needs and your family's preferences.
